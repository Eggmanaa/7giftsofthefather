Archetype 23 of 35 · The Heart-Centered Connectors (Connection-Dominant Triads)

The Encouraging Shepherd

To lead teams toward a goal by fostering a culture of profound psychological safety, connection, and inspiration.

EnthusiastStrategistLover
Axis signature: 1 Why · 2 Who

The Enthusiast inspires, the Strategist leads, and the Lover cares. This chord builds teams on psychological safety, guiding toward a goal while making everyone feel they belong.

At Their Best

The leader people would follow anywhere. They pair motivating vision, clear direction, and genuine care, creating cultures where people feel safe enough to do their best work.

Under Stress · The Shadow

The Enthusiast avoids hard truths, the Strategist quietly steers, and the Lover cannot bear anyone's disappointment. The tell: a leader so committed to everyone feeling good that accountability disappears and the goal drifts.

The Core Tension

Between keeping the team happy and holding them to the standard the goal requires.

Where They Thrive
  • People-first team leadership
  • Culture and morale building
  • Coaching toward shared goals
  • Leading with empathy and vision
Growth Edge

They grow by having the hard conversation, learning that real care sometimes sounds like a clear expectation.

In Relationship

Playing Alongside the Seven Gifts

Natural Harmony

Those who lead with Service and Teaching, who supply the execution and substance behind the vision.

Creative Friction

Those who lead with Prophecy, whose directness can feel like a threat to the safety the Shepherd is building.

"Encourage one another and build each other up."1 Thessalonians 5:11

Embodied by · Timothy, the young pastor urged to lead his people with gentleness, conviction, and genuine care.

Archetype 13 of 35 · The Visionary Mentors (Vision & Heart Hybrids)

The Wise Shepherd

To lead with profound wisdom and a clear plan, while providing deep, pastoral care for the team.

EruditeStrategistLover
Axis signature: 2 Why · 1 Who

The Erudite brings wisdom, the Strategist brings a clear path, and the Lover brings pastoral warmth. This chord leads people somewhere worth going and cares for them the whole way.

At Their Best

The leader people trust with their souls. They combine sound judgment, a real plan, and deep compassion, so guidance feels safe rather than driven, and direction comes wrapped in care.

Under Stress · The Shadow

The Erudite withdraws into analysis, the Strategist quietly controls, and the Lover absorbs the team's pain until it cannot lead. The tell: a shepherd so attuned to everyone's feelings that no decision gets made, or one who intellectualizes care until it stops being felt.

The Core Tension

Between the clarity to lead decisively and the tenderness to move at the pace of the wounded.

Where They Thrive
  • Pastoral and organizational leadership
  • Counseling-informed management
  • Guiding teams through change with care
  • People-first strategy
Growth Edge

They grow by making the hard call even when it disappoints, trusting that clear direction is itself a form of care.

In Relationship

Playing Alongside the Seven Gifts

Natural Harmony

Those who lead with Service and Giving, who turn wise, caring direction into tangible provision.

Creative Friction

Those who lead with Prophecy, whose urgency to confront can feel like a threat to the safety the Shepherd is building.

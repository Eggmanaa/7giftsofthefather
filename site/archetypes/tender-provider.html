Archetype 18 of 35 · The Bedrock Builders (Action-Dominant Triads)

The Tender Provider

To express deep compassion by tirelessly working and generously giving to meet the tangible needs of the hurting.

ServantHostLover
Axis signature: 2 How · 1 Who

The Servant acts, the Host provides, and the Lover feels. This chord meets suffering with practical, generous, tangible care, love you can hold in your hands.

At Their Best

Compassion that shows up with groceries. They pair hard work, real resources, and deep empathy, so hurting people are not just comforted but actually cared for, in body and in heart.

Under Stress · The Shadow

The Servant over-gives, the Host tries to control the help, and the Lover over-identifies until it burns out. The tell: a caregiver pouring out until empty, unable to receive, quietly drowning in others' needs.

The Core Tension

Between the boundless desire to help and the finite body doing the helping.

Where They Thrive
  • Caregiving, chaplaincy, relief work
  • Practical mercy ministries
  • Meeting tangible needs of the hurting
  • Hospitality to the suffering
Growth Edge

They grow by accepting their own limits, learning that a well-placed no is what keeps their yes alive.

In Relationship

Playing Alongside the Seven Gifts

Natural Harmony

Those who lead with Teaching and Leadership, who help the care scale without losing its heart.

Creative Friction

Those who lead with Prophecy, whose hard truths can feel harsh to the Tender Provider's protective warmth.

"She opens her arms to the poor and extends her hands to the needy."Proverbs 31:20

Embodied by · Dorcas (Tabitha), always doing good and helping the poor, who sewed garments for the widows with her own hands.

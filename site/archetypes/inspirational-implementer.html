Archetype 32 of 35 · The Integrated Hybrids (Balanced Triads)

The Inspirational Implementer

To lead teams to execute a plan with excellence by fostering a positive, high-morale, and collaborative environment.

ServantEnthusiastStrategist

The Inspirational Implementer is a leader who excels at making work both productive and positive. This archetype blends the Strategist's clear, goal-oriented vision, the Servant's commitment to practical excellence and follow-through, and the Enthusiast's talent for building morale and fostering collaboration. They are the ultimate team captain, able to get the team aligned on a game plan, ensure everyone executes their role with diligence, and keep the energy high from kickoff to the final whistle.

The synergy of this triad is a recipe for a high-performing, high-morale team. The Strategist provides the clear and compelling "what" and "why." The Servant provides the model for the "how," demonstrating a commitment to quality and a willingness to do whatever it takes. The Enthusiast fosters the "who," building the relational bonds and positive culture that make the team want to work together. They are the "Master Implementer" and the "Inspirational Leader" in one. They naturally resolve the tension between the Strategist's focus on the agenda and the Enthusiast's desire for connection by making connection part of the agenda.

They are exceptional project managers, department heads, and coaches. They have a gift for turning around underperforming teams by simultaneously improving both the processes and the culture. The tension they must manage is between the Strategist's drive for results and the Servant's focus on doing things perfectly. The Enthusiast's influence can sometimes lead to a focus on team harmony at the expense of confronting poor performance. Their growth as a leader comes from learning to have difficult accountability conversations in a way that is both direct (Strategist) and affirming of the person's value (Enthusiast).
